 Please read through this information carefully before contacting me with a request!

these are for character illustrations!
I will draw anything except NSFW (violence, sexual content).
I like a challenge, but please keep in mind that some things I may not have ever drawn before.
I do reserve the right to decline a commission request for any reason.

references are REQUIRED!
I want to make sure I fulfill your request to the best of my ability!
Direct visual references are preferred, but written descriptions, moodboards, and photographs are okay as well.
Anything that helps get the point across.

the process:
Send me an email with the subject [commission request] and the following details filled out:

- <Your preferred name>
- Preferred Contact: <platform> (<handle>)
- Preferred Payment: <platform> (<handle>)
- <character name(s)> as <chibi, bust, or fullbody>
- Style: <flats, soft-cel, or painted>
- BG: <simple, any preferred details>
- References: <direct images, links, or written descriptions>

email: jacqueline.l.aquino@gmail.com

If you have a request that doesn't quite fit the specifications listed,
feel free to send me a message regardless and we'll see if we can figure something out together.

I will get back to you by your preferred method of contact accepting or denying the request.
If accepting, I will also write the final price to confirm.

A down payment of about 20 % of the final price is required before I begin working.
If anything happens and you no longer want your commission after I have begun working,
this down payment will not be returned.

I will send updates periodically.
At minimum, once for composition & design approval, and once more for color approval.

After this point, the full payment is required before I continue working.

I'll message you once more with the download link when I'm finished!

Feel free to use the art in any way, but please credit me as the artist!
