I am a digital animator and illustrator with a BS in CS, minors in Visual Arts and Psychology, and a passion for storytelling through animation. During my time in undergrad, I discovered a passion for sharing my knowledge with others -- I enjoy teaching people the foundations of animated filmmaking while creating an environment in which people can be themselves and enjoy working together.
I am very aware of how limited my own knowledgebase is, however,
and I am always trying and learning new things!

jacqueline.l.aquino@gmail.com












I've been in a longterm struggle with self-doubt and imposter syndrome, and I feel that being open about these feelings while simultaneously acknowledging that we all must keep pushing ourselves forward can help others face their own daily demons in their own ways.

I am also in a longterm struggle against run-on sentences, but I don't claim to be a writer.
I mainly create what I enjoy, and I hope that you'll enjoy yourself as well!

An aside: this is my first website, built from scratch using html, bootstrap css, and a dash of javascript!
